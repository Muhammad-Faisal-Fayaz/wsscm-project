Who We Are

Serving Mardan With Pride

The Government of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa established WSSCM as an independent corporate utility company in 2016, taking over water supply, wastewater and solid waste management from Tehsil Municipal Administration.

Safe and reliable water for domestic and commercial use across Mardan.
