What We Do

Waste Water Management

Protecting public health and the environment through effective wastewater treatment.

Sewer Network

Comprehensive underground sewer infrastructure covering all major areas of Mardan for efficient wastewater collection.

Treatment Plants

Modern wastewater treatment facilities processing sewage before safe discharge, protecting rivers and groundwater.

Drain Jetting

High-pressure jetting services to unblock and clean drains, maintaining optimal sewer network performance.

Overflow Prevention

Proactive monitoring and maintenance to prevent sewage overflow events that can harm communities.

Effluent Monitoring

Regular testing of treated effluent to ensure environmental compliance and prevent pollution.

Desludging Service

Tanker services for septic tank desludging in areas without direct sewer connections.
