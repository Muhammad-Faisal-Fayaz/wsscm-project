What We Do

Water Supply Management

Delivering safe, clean drinking water to every household across Mardan.

Distribution Network

Extensive pipeline network ensuring clean water reaches every part of Mardan city and surrounding areas.

Water Treatment

Modern treatment facilities purifying water to meet national and international safety standards before supply.

Pressure Management

24/7 monitoring and pressure management ensuring consistent water supply at adequate pressure levels.

Quality Testing

Regular lab testing of water samples from across the network to verify potability and safety compliance.

Leak Repair

Rapid response teams deployed to identify and repair pipeline leaks, minimizing water loss and disruption.

New Connections

Streamlined new connection process for residential and commercial properties, with online application support.
