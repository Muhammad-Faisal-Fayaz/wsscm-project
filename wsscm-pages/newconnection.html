New Connection Application

Getting a new water connection is simple. Complete the form, submit required documents, and our team will process your application within 7–14 working days.

1

Fill Application Form

Complete the online form with your property and personal details.

2

Submit Documents

CNIC copy, property ownership proof, and site plan.

3

Site Inspection

WSSCM engineers will visit to assess feasibility and plan the connection.

4

Connection Installed

After fee payment, connection is installed and your meter is activated.
